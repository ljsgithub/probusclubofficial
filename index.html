What is the Falkirk Probus Club?

Welcome to the Falkirk Probus Club. Our club provides retired professionals and business men the opportunity to meet on a regular basis to talk about topics of interest. There may be key speakers that have been chosen to present to the group based around the topic of discussion that month.

Where do we meet and how often?

The Falkirk Probus Club meets every second Tuesday in The Falkirk Stadium, Westfield, third floor, commencing at 10.00 with socialising over coffee, tea and biscuits.

Meetings start at 10.30 with guest speakers providing insight into subjects of topical and local interest. Coffee refreshments are available from 10 am.

For those of you who do not know Falkirk very well, it is an beautiful town that has alot of historical landmarks. Ranging from the Antonine Wall to Monuments.

Head to our 'About Us' page where you can find more information regarding the Falkirk ProBus club.



What do we talk about?

We pre-arrange a variety of interesting subjects to discuss at our Falkirk Probus Club meetings. Some of our upcoming talks will be about H.M.I.C.S, Networld Rail, Order of St John, My Kind of Music and much more.

*Regular lunches are also enjoyed.

We are very open to suggestions, so if there is anything you'd like to discuss, we'd be more than happy to hear your ideas.
